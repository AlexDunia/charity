    In April 2020, and for the second year running, the
    Norwegian Relief Council classified the conflict in Southern Cameroons as the most neglected conflict in
    the world. The implications for victims of this
    conflict are dire. As a consequence of this neglect,
    there are minimal efforts by the international community to address the root causes of the conflict
    and bring about sustainable peace.

    Eleanora’s foundation was founded on February 18th, 2015 by Nora Dunia, a graduate in Business Education (BEd), Delta state university, Abraka. Eleanora’s foundation is dedicated to serving society in different ways but the major functions include providing a helping hand in the eradication of poverty among the children, the widows, and the youths.

    The question we ask ourselves every day is how can we give to society? What sectors in the society need to be improved and what parts of the people in the society get less response to their priorities? How can we help these people even if it’s at the very least empowering them with top skills that when mastered with time, would pave way for a better life for them and their offspring?
